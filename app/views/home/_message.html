Aug 2018:

The proceedings of the 27th International Conference on Computational Linguistics (COLING 2018) and its 12 associated workshops are now available in the ACL Anthology. Also, the proceedings of the 19th Annual Meeting of the Special Interest Group on Discourse and Dialogue (SigDial 2018) are now available on the ACL Anthology. Finally, the proceedings of the 56rd Annual Meeting of the Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL 2018) and its 11 associated workshops are now available on the ACL Anthology.
